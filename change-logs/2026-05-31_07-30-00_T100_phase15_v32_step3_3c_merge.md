## 7. 手动操作提醒(Win 端)

```sql
SELECT 
  tag, COUNT(*) AS n,
  COUNT(DISTINCT payload->>'seat') AS distinct_seats
FROM diagnostic_events
WHERE tag LIKE 'pattern_d.ocr2_%'
  AND occurred_at > NOW() - INTERVAL '30 min'
GROUP BY tag;
-- 预期 tag = pattern_d.ocr2_action_fallback / pattern_d.ocr2_amount_fallback
-- 触发率应跟 silent fold rate 反相关(fallback 越多 = OCR-2 真填补越多)
```
